On the night of October 30th 2023, at least 22 members of al-Badrasawi family that had been displaced from al-Bureji camp, including at least four women and between two and six children, were killed and a large number of civilians were wounded in an alleged Israeli airstrike on a home near the medical services headquarters in the Al-Zawaida area of the Gaza Strip.

Initial reports by Al Jazeera the day after the incident reported that 15 civilians were killed, mostly women and children.
