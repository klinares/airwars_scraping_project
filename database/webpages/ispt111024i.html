Airwars assessment

Shortly before 3 PM on Friday, October 11th 2024, alleged Israeli quadcopter drone gunfire hit Al Fouqa School, which housed displaced civilians, located behind Abu Rashid Pool in Jabalia Refugee Camp, injuring at least 15 civilians.

Twitter/X users Quds Press Agency (@qudspressagency) and the media network Gaza Now (@nowgnna) were the first to report the incident between 15:10 and 15:20.

At least 15 civilians were reported injured and transported to the Kamal Adwan Hospital in three batches. The incident was extensively covered online by news outlets and social media users.
